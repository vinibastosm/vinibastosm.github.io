About

I am a computational physicist and PhD student in Experimental and Theoretical Physics at IFSC-USP, funded by FAPESP. My research focuses on nanophotonics and light–matter interaction in complex structures such as metasurfaces. I work on numerical simulations of electromagnetic and quantum systems, combined with machine learning techniques, mainly Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) and Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), for the modeling and inverse design of nanophotonic devices.

Research Interests

  • Light–matter interaction
  • Quantum Nanophotonics
  • Plasmonics
  • Deep Learning
  • Physics-Informed Machine Learning
  • Numerical Simulations
